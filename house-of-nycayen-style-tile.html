04Rules of the house
Imagery: hair as medium, sculpture, material, light — never a person, a face, or a fabricated result.
Motion: scroll-driven, never scroll-jacked; one bold move per screen; prefers-reduced-motion fully respected.
Contrast: ivory ≈16:1, muted ≈8:1 on ink. Re-verify any new gold-on-color text at ≥4.5:1.
Restraint: the iridescent sheen is the single indulgence — everything around it stays quiet and editorial.
